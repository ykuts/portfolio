Nice to meet you -

My name is Yuliia and this is my story...

I am a young cheerful girl who finds joy in the simple pleasures of life.

My passion for traveling has taken me to amazing parts of the world where I am immersed in different cultures and enjoy the beauty of diversity.

When I'm not exploring new places, I love to do handmade crafts. There is something incredibly enjoyable about creating something with your own hands. Whether it's a piece of jewelry or an interior piece, the process of creating brings me a sense of fulfillment and peace.

I believe that life is an adventure and I am always interested in discovering new places, meeting interesting people and discovering the beauty of the unknown.

Join me on this journey where every day is an opportunity to explore, create and enjoy the miracles of life.
